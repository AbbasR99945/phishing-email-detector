# Pseudocode

1. Read the email pasted by the user or load one of the fake training samples.
2. Search for pressure language, credential requests, and suspicious sender wording.
3. Extract every URL and inspect its hostname.
4. Add risk points for shortened links, lookalike brands, IP hosts, and missing HTTPS.
5. Convert the total into low, suspicious, or high risk.
6. Show the findings, link notes, triage guidance, and export option.

Review note: the score should always be paired with visible evidence, because awareness tools are more useful when the reasoning is clear.
